You are entitled to a widow's and widower's pension for surviving dependants under the following conditions
widower's pension for surviving dependants:

  * Death of the (spouse) partner as a result of an insurance event: 
    * Accident at work,
    * commuting accident or
    * occupational disease
  * You were already living in a legally valid marriage or civil partnership in accordance with the Civil Partnership Act.

As a rule, you are not entitled if

  * you only entered into the marriage or civil partnership after the insured event and
  * the death occurred within the first year of this marriage or civil partnership.
