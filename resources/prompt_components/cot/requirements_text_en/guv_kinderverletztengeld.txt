You are entitled to child injury benefit if:

  * you have to supervise, look after or care for a child in your household due to the consequences of an insured event (school and kindergarten accident),
  * your child has not yet reached the age of 12,
  * you were entitled to a wage or other cash benefit (e.g. sickness benefit, unemployment benefit) before you supervised, looked after or cared for your child and
  * no other person living in your household can look after your child.
